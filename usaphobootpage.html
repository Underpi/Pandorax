USAPhO Bootcamp

Class Schedule: 12 classes from 7/13 - 8/21 every Sunday and Wednesday 8:30-9:30 PM EST (classes will be recorded)

Description: This bootcamp will prepare students for the F=ma exam, the entrance exam to the USAPhO, assuming no prior experience with physics. This bootcamp will cover all of physics mechanics, as well as general techniques to solve difficult and tricky problems. By the end of the bootcamp, students should have enough knowledge to pass the F=ma, qualifying for the prestigious USAPhO exam.

Prerequisites: Basic understanding of algebra and elementary trigonometry; no prior physics knowledge necessary.
