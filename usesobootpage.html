USESO Bootcamp

Class Schedule: 12 classes from 9/16 - 12/9 every Sunday 6:30-7:30 PM EST (classes will be recorded)

Description: This bootcamp prepares students for the first round of the US Earth Science Olympiad, assuming no prior experience with Earth science. This bootcamp covers all four spheres of Earth: geosphere, hydrosphere, atmosphere, and astronomy. By the end of the bootcamp, students should have enough knowledge to reach the national training camp of the USESO.

Prerequisites: None, but a basic understanding of high school algebra is recommended.
