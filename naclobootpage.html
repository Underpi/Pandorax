NACLO Bootcamp

Class Schedule: 12 classes from 7/13 - 8/21 every Sunday and Wednesday 7-8 PM EST (classes will be recorded)

Description: This bootcamp will prepare students for the NACLO open round, assuming no prior experience with linguistics or linguistics olympiads. We will cover fundamental topics in linguistics with a special focus on problem-solving, including Phonology, Syntax, Semantics and Morphology. By the end of the bootcamp, students should have enough knowledge to qualify for the prestigious NACLO Invitationals exam.

Prerequisites: None, although a knack for solving logic puzzles is expected.
