USABO Bootcamp

Class Schedule: 12 classes from 11/23 - 2/1 every Sunday 8-9 PM EST (classes will be recorded)

Description: NOTE: this is an advanced USABO bootcamp. This bootcamp will prepare students for the USABO semifinal round, covering 5 important units critical in reaching top 50 and camp: Biochemistry, Cell Biology, Genetics, Plants, and Animal Physiology. By the end of the bootcamp, students should have enough knowledge to reach top 50 or higher at the USABO semifinal round.

Prerequisites: None.
