According to Greek mythology, Pandora's box was a magical artifact that trapped a multitude of all troubles and sorrows in the world, which were released upon opening of this box, leaving only hope trapped inside. Knowledge, in a similar way, could bring advancement and peace to society, or also trouble â€” if used by the wrong hands. At Pandorax, we teach the knowledge one needs to fulfill their dream in STEM, setting them up for a path to realize their potential and impact the world as future scientists and innovators. With nearly 1,000 pages of written guides and numerous bootcamps, our own Pandora's box is here to guide you to your potential. As for how you use this newfound knowledge, that we leave up to you.

Executive Team

Founder & CEO

Aiden Pan

Aiden led the recruitment and development of the Olympiad Guides. Without a mentor, he is a published biomed researcher, ISEF finalist in Physics, USESO national finalist, VEX Robotics World Championship Top 20, and captain of his high school's nationally-ranked science olympiad team, who (emotionally) claimed the title of NY state champions in 2021.

Chief Operating Officer

Amit Saha

Amit leads the development of the mentorship program of Pandorax. Previously, he pursued research at multiple university laboratories and was recognized as a Regeneron STS Scholar and Grand Award winner at ISEF. As COO of Pandorax, Amit seeks to combine his expertise in research with his passion for scientific education to bring research to all students.

Chief Technology Officer

Arjun Krish

Arjun was the sole coder of the Pandorax website. He placed in the USABO Top 50 as a freshman and conducts research in neuroscience and ophthalmology at Stanford University. He leads his school's Science Journalism club and is the biology main of their science bowl team that (hopes) to go to nats again.

Chief Marketing Officer

David Zhou

David qualified for the US International Earth Science Olympiad Team in 10th grade, where he placed 2nd internationally on the individual test and received a gold medal. He also loves to compete in STEM competitions, captaining his high school's science bowl team, which he led to become national champions in 2023.

Chief Human Resources Officer

Aryan Bora

Aryan has been fascinated by STEM throughout his entire life. He is a USABO National Finalist, USAJMO winner, USACO Platinum Division competitor, USNCO Honors awardee, USAPhO Bronze Medal recipient, and is currently conducting research in math at MIT PRIMES-USA. As the CHRO at Pandorax, Aryan hopes to share his love of STEM with other science enthusiasts.
