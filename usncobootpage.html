USNCO Bootcamp

Class Schedule: 12 classes from 9/16 - 12/9 every Sunday 5-6 PM EST (classes will be recorded)

Description: This bootcamp will prepare students for the USNCO open exam, assuming no prior experience with chemistry. This bootcamp will cover the chemistry of reactions, acids and bases, electronic structure, states of matter, thermodynamics, and more. By the end of the bootcamp, students should have enough knowledge to pass the USNCO open and win honors at the national level.

Prerequisites: Basic understanding of algebra; no prior chemistry knowledge required.
